In this game you will make a treasure hunt style game, where you explore the tilemap and collect chests. A link to the completed game is here:

https://arcade.makecode.com/S36053-64720-95109-55444

2.1, Navigate to https://arcade.makecode.com

2.2, Click "New Project"

2.3, Then name your project, and press create.

2.4, Once your screen looks like this, you can continue on to the next step.

In this step you will add a sprite to the scene.

3.1, For this project you will need a sprite, a sprite is a character that you control, however it can also be items or projectiles.

3.2, Open up the sprites menu, then select the "Set Mysprite to sprite of kind player" block.

3.3, Then drag that block to underneath the green On Start block. The on start block runs as soon as the play button is pressed.

3.4, You see that grey box? If you click it, you will see the options of sprites you can pick from.

3.5, Click on the button that says Gallery.

3.6, Scroll through and pick a sprite that you like, this will be the explorer in our treasure hunt.

3.7, Click on the sprite that you want, then click Done (in the bottom right corner).

In this step you will add motion to the sprite that you just created.

4.1, Open the Controller menu.

4.2, Drag and drop the "Move mySprite with buttons" block underneath the block which added the sprite.

4.3, Press play on the little gamepad to see your game in action.

4.4, Use the D-pad to control the sprite, but try leaving the screen. It works, and the camera doesn't follow you. This is a problem because the sprite can get lost.

In this step you will add a tile map, this step will take a bit longer because you have to design a module.

Make sure your tile map has the following.

  • Border around the edge.
  • Walls inside to make a maze.
